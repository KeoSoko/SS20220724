Potential Features to Enhance Simple Slips
1. Advanced Analytics & Reporting
•	Custom Date Range Reports: Allow users to generate reports for specific date ranges
•	Category-Based Spending Breakdown: Visualize spending patterns by category with pie/bar charts
•	Spending Trends: Show month-over-month or year-over-year spending trends
•	Tax Deduction Summaries: Automatically flag and summarize potential tax-deductible expenses
2. Enhanced Receipt Management
•	Bulk Actions: Select multiple receipts to tag, export, or delete at once
•	Customizable Tags/Categories: Let users create and manage their own tags and categories
•	Search & Filter Improvements: Add robust search by amount, date range, store name, etc.
•	Receipt Notes: Allow users to add personal notes to receipts for context
3. Budget & Financial Management
•	Budget Tracking: Set monthly budgets by category and track progress
•	Financial Goals: Set savings targets and track progress toward them
•	Expense Alerts: Get notifications when spending in a category exceeds budget
•	Recurring Expense Recognition: Identify subscription payments and regular expenses
4. Integration & Sharing
•	Export Options: Add PDF, CSV, and Excel export formats for reports
•	Email Reports: Schedule and send periodic reports via email
•	Sharing Capabilities: Share expenses with family members or accountants
•	Calendar Integration: Sync receipt dates with calendar for better expense tracking
5. Enhanced OCR & Data Extraction
•	Item-Level Extraction: Parse individual line items from receipts automatically
•	Currency Conversion: For international receipts, convert to the user's preferred currency
•	Vendor Recognition: Build a database of commonly visited stores to improve recognition
•	Receipt Correction Tool: Allow users to easily fix OCR mistakes
6. User Experience Improvements
•	Onboarding Flow: Create a guided tour for new users
•	Dashboard Customization: Let users arrange dashboard widgets to their preference
•	Dark Mode: Add a dark theme option
•	Offline Support: Allow scanning and basic functions without internet connection
